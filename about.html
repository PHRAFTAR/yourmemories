Tum wo ladki ho jisne mujhe safe aur comfortable feel karwaya. Lekin tumne mujhe itna comfortable bana diya ki main tumse pyaar karne laga. Tum thodi si chubby ho, aur mujhe tumhare har ek cheez bahut pasand hai—tumhara soft aur cute hona mujhe hamesha ek panda yaad dilata hai. Jab main tumhe apne haathon mein uthata hoon, toh mujhe lagta hai ki main tumhe hamesha protect kar sakta hoon. Jab bhi tumhare haath pakadta hoon, lagta hai main apni life ki sabse sahi jagah pe hoon. Jab tumhe hug karta hoon, toh aisa lagta hai jaise maine duniya jeet li ho. Tumhare paas hona mere liye ek achievement hai. Haan, tum kabhi-kabhi thodi rude bhi ho, par tumhari kindness uss rudeness ke neeche hamesha chamakti hai. Tumne jo mujhe pyaar diya hai, uska koi end nahi hai, aur yeh main tumhari aankhon mein dekh sakta hoon. Jab main tumhari aankhon mein dekhta hoon, toh mujhe apna future nazar aata hai—meri zindagi tumhare saath nazar aati hai. Maine tumhe bohot dard diya hai, aur wo main kabhi theek nahi kar sakta. Tumhara dil toda, aur mujhe pata hai koi bhi sorry us dard ko door nahi kar sakta. Par main tumse maafi mangne aur punishment ke liye tayar hoon, aur main tumhe ye sabit karne ke liye tayar hoon ki main wo insaan hoon jo tum deserve karti ho. Main tumhe kabhi nahi khona chahta. Jab maine apni life mein tumhe dekha hai, toh maine hum dono ka future socha hai—ek saath grow karte hue, apne galtiyon se seekhte hue, aur khoobsurat yaadein banate hue. Maine tumhe dekha hai, hamari beti ko tumhare haathon mein, aur mujhe lagta hai ki tumhare bina wo future impossible hai. Tumne mujhe yeh promise kiya tha, aur main us promise ko kabhi bhi todne nahi dunga. Koi bhi qeemat ho, main jo bhi kar sakta hoon, main karunga tumhe apni zindagi mein rakhne ke liye, aur humari future daughter ke liye
